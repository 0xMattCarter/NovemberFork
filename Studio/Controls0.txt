Intro Commands

K:                  Toggle Instructions,
W, A, S, & D:       Change direction and speed,
0:                  Make all particles still,
X:                  Wipe all particles from canvas and toggle all modes off,

M:                  Spawn single particle at curser (clicking does this as well),
N:                  Remove most recently spawned particle,
Z:                  Spawn original number of particles,
1, 2, 3:            Spawn smaller quantities of particles,

Commands

U:                  Make point spawn closer 
J:                  Make point spawn further (if point spawn is set to 0, it will set the z-index to random)

Modes

C:                  Toggle Connecting mode,
E:                  Toggle Etheracting mode (only works with < 100 particles on the canvas), 
T:                  Toggle Trail mode,
